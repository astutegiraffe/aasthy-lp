How does it work?

 

Asset shortlisted

After conducting rigorous market review, with due diligence to target returns and legal title, we shortlisted this Grade 'A' asset.

 

Investment booked

Investor books the investment by paying 1% of the investment amount, this is done to ensure that minimum required investors are met before buying the shortlisted asset.

 

Play the waiting game

Investor waits for the investment opportunity to be fully booked (7-10 days).

 

Investment completed

Investor transfers the remaining 99% of the investment amount on receipt of a communication from Aasthy.

 

Debentures issued

Aasthy creates a special purpose vehicle (SPV) for the shortlisted asset, in which all the investors will be debenture holders. Debentures will be issued corresponding to the investment amount.

 

Asset purchased

The special purpose vehicle (SPV) purchases the asset in its name using the amount collected from investors.

 

Asset sold

The entity exits from the investment by selling the asset and settling all the associated expenses to third parties.

 

Payout of proceedings

The entity pays out the proceedings from selling the asset (after all deductions) to all the investors as interest on Debentures.
